3 cups cooked, chilled white rice (jasmine or basmati work best)
3 large eggs, lightly beaten
1 cup mixed vegetables (diced carrots, peas, and sweet corn)
3-4 tablespoons neutral cooking oil (canola, vegetable, or peanut)
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon sesame oil
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup green onions (scallions), chopped
Instructions:
1. Prep your ingredients
2. Scramble the eggs
3. Saute the veggies
4. Fry the rice
5. Season & Combine
